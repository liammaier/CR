APPENDIX A

COGNITIVE, LANGUAGE AND READING ASSESSMENT

Note the results of the comprehensive battery were not directly related to the research questions and are not reported as part of this dissertation. The assessments were completed over two sessions (one and four), as outlined in Figure 1 in the Chapter III. Below is a list of the assessment measures given as part of the battery.

Session 1: Cognitive Assessment

  • Attention and working memory
    • Woodcock Johnson III: Visual matching (Timed: 2 minutes x 2)
    • Woodcock Johnson III: Numbers reversed (5 minutes)
    • Paced Auditory Serial Addition Test (PASAT)
  • Verbal learning and recall
    • California Verbal Language Test (10 min, 5 min)
    • Woodcock Johnson III: Memory for words (5 minutes)
  • Executive processing
    • Woodcock Johnson III: Planning subtest (10 minutes)
  • Cognitive efficiency
    • Woodcock Johnson III: Decision speed (Timed: 3 minutes)
    • Speed of Comprehension Test (SCOLP) (Timed portion - 2 minutes; untimed portion â€“ 10 minutes)
