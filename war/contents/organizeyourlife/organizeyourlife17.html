Chapter 17: Synchronization via iTunes

You will almost certainly want to synchronize calendars, contacts, music, movies, etc. between your device and your desktop or laptop computer. Basically, synchronization refers to the ability to transfer content between your iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch and your computer and to keep that information “in sync” or matching. These functions can be performed manually by connecting your device to a computer via a cable or they can be done directly from the iOS 5 device via iCloud.

To Initiate Synchronization

  • Plug the USB end of the sync cable into a computer.
  • Plug the other end of the cable into the bottom of the iPod Touch.
  • You will be asked to install iTunes.
  • Once iTunes is installed, select your device from the “Devices” section of the Library located on the left side of the iTunes screen (see Figure 17-1). This will open the Summary tab of the sync settings.

As you will see once you select your device, there are several tabs at the top of the screen. The bottom of the iTunes screen will remain the same, regardless of the tab selected (see Figure 17-2). This section of the screen shows the total capacity of the device, the amount of space taken up by various file types (audio, photos, apps, and other), and the amount of space available (free space).

In addition, the device can be manually synchronized by selecting the Sync button on the right side of the bottom section.

This example shows a Windows-based computer. Options will vary on a Mac computer. In addition, you can synchronize via Wi-Fi using iCloud.

To Synchronize Contacts

  • Click the Info tab.
  • Click the checkbox next to the Sync Contacts With field in the “Contacts” section of the screen.
  • Choose the desired option from the drop-down list (see Figure 17-15).
  • Choose to sync All Contacts or Selected Groups. Select desired groups, if appropriate.
  • Click Apply in the lower right corner to sync.

To Synchronize Calendars

  • Click the Info tab.
  • Click the checkbox next to the Sync Calendars With field in the “Calendars” section of the screen.
  • Choose the desired option from the drop-down list (see Figure 17-15).
  • Choose to sync All Calendars or Selected Calendars. Select the desired calendars, if appropriate.
  • Place a check in the checkbox next to Do Not Sync Events Older Than 30 Days if you do not want to sync your entire calendar. [Note: 30 days is the default; you can change the number of days according to your needs.]
  • Click Apply in the lower right corner to sync.

To Synchronize Bookmarks

  • Click the Info tab.
  • Click the checkbox next to the Sync Bookmarks With field in the “Web Browser” section of the screen.
  • Choose the desired option from the drop-down list.
  • Click Apply in the lower right corner to sync.
