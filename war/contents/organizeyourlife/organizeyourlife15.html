Weather

The Weather app provides local weather forecasts (see Figure 15-1).

In addition, you can add cities to your weather list. To Add Cities to the Weather App

  • Tap the Weather app icon on the Home screen. The local weather should appear.
  • Tap information icon in the lower right corner.
  • Tap the plus icon in the upper left corner to add a city (see Figure 15-2).
  • Enter the desired city, state, or zip code.
  • Tap Search, if necessary.
  • Tap the desired city. It will automatically be added to your Weather page list.
  • Tap Done.
  • Flick from page to page to see the weather for the cities you have selected.

  • Open Weather.
  • Enter at least three cities on the Weather page.
  • Flick to see the weather in each city.

To Delete Cities from the Weather App

  • Tap the Weather app icon on the Home screen.
  • Tap information icon in the lower right corner.
  • Tap the minus sign icon to left of the to-be-deleted city name.
  • Tap Delete to the right of the to-be-deleted city name.
  • Tap Done.

Siri Basics

Siri allows you to get current weather forecasts by speaking. To interact with the Weather app using Siri:

  1. Press and hold the Home Button until “What can I help you with?” and the microphone appear on the screen. Tap the microphone to turn it on if it is not blinking.
  2. Siri will understand a number of verbal commands, such as:
    1. What’s the weather in San Francisco?
    2. What’s todays weather?

GIVE IT A TRY!

  • Check the local weather forecast using Siri.

Stocks

The Stocks app provides specific stock information (see Figure 15-3).

In addition, you can modify the list of stocks by deleting default stocks and adding your own choices to the list. In addition, important stock news of the day appears at the bottom of the screen.

To Add Stocks to the Stocks List

  • Tap the Stocks app icon on the Home screen.
  • Tap the information icon in the lower right corner.
  • Tap the plus icon in the upper left corner to add a stock (see Figure 15-4).
  • Enter the desired stock code.
  • If you don’t know the code, enter the company name in the Search field, tap Search, then tap the desired stock code from the search results list.
  • The chosen stock will automatically be added to your Stocks list.
  • Tap Done.
  • Scroll up or down to see entire list of stocks.

To Play a YouTube Video

  • Tap the YouTube app icon on the Home screen.
  • Use the Featured, Most Viewed, or Search button to find a video to view.
  • Select the desired video reference on the screen by tapping it.
  • Tap the play arrow (right arrow in the center) on the playback screen.
  • While viewing the video, you can bring back the controls (pause, rewind, fast forward, etc.) by tapping on the screen (see Figure 15-9).
  • Tap Done in the upper left corner when finished viewing the video.
