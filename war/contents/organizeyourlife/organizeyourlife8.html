Contacts Settings

As you may have noticed, the default display for contacts is first name, then last name. There are four settings that can be changed within the Contacts section.

  • Sort Order (the order in which contacts are alphabetized, i.e., by first name or by last name)
  • Display Order (the way contacts are displayed on the screen, with first name first or last name first, e.g., “Michelle Wild” or “Wild Michelle”)
  • My Info (allows you to identify yourself in Contacts)
  • Default Account (allows you to assign an account such as iCloud to which your Contacts will be saved automatically) [Note: You will not see this Setting option if you have no such account established.]

To Change the Display and/or Sort Order of Contacts

  • Tap Settings from the Home screen.
  • Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars (see Figure 8-33).
  • Tap and drag to the Contacts section (see Figure 8-34).
  • Tap the Sort Order or Display Order field to modify the setting.
  • Tap the desired order (see Figure 8-35).
  • Tap located in the upper left corner.
  • Tap located in the upper left corner.
  • Tap the Home button.
