Unit 6 - Water and air temperature - Investigation - 2 - Temperature of mixing air

Mix fluids and measure temperature changes

This unit addresses NSES standards for inquiry and physical science at grades 5-8 and NCTM standards on Algebra and Data Analysis at grades 6-8.

sensors: temperature

Discovery Question: What happens to the temperature in the room when you open the window?

Students use a temperature sensor to measure air temperature. They explore changes in classroom air temperature as a function of time and position, when a window is opened. The emphasis is on methods of inquiry.
